Problem Statement:
Find the intersection of two sorted arrays. OR in other words, Given 2 sorted arrays,
find all the elements which occur in both the arrays.

Example 1:
Input:
A: [1 2 3 3 4 5 6], B: [3 3 5]
Output: 3,3,5
Explanation: We are given two arrays A and B. The elements present in both the arrays
are 3,3 and 5.

Example 2:
Input:
A: [1 2 3 3 4 5 6], B: [3 5]
Output: 3,5
Explanation: We are given two arrays A and B. The elements present in both the arrays
are 3 and 5.
